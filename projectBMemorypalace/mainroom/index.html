About the Project

This project is a digital representation of my memory palace, specifically focusing on my bedroom. It allows you to navigate through the room, exploring different sections and their associated memories.

  • To move around in the room, use your arrow keys or the buttons on screen.
  • You can replace the furniture around the room by dragging them.
  • Click on the furniture to reveal their description.
