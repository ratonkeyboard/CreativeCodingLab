This project is a digital representation of my memory palace, specifically focusing on my bedroom. It allows you to navigate through the room, exploring different sections and their associated memories.

1. To move around in the room, use your arrow keys or the buttons on screen

2. You can replace the furnitures around the room by dragging them

3. Click on the furniture to reveal their description
